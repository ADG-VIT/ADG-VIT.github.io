  • iOS Fusion

    iOS Fusion was a 15 hours event, conducted on 10th – 11th September 2016 and speaker Mustafa.

    Participants were taught basics of Swift and given a hands on session on how to build an iOS app on Xcode.

    The event spanned two days, where the first day, an insight was given in the App Development World and the next day a basic app was built.

  • UniDev

    Unidev was all about Virtual Reality, where a software called Vuforia was incorporated.

    The event was held on 18th September, 2016 by Speaker Mantej for about 8 hours.

    Participants were given the task of building an App that used VR as a tool to showcase beautiful 3D objects in an augmented environment.

  • App-a-Thon

    App-A-Thon is a premium event of Apple Developers Group which is conducted every year.

    It was last held on 16th and 17th April, 2016.

    It is a workshop/competition where students were given a technical session previously and later had to compete against each other. Prizes and certificates were given for Best UI/UX, Best App and Best Overall App.

  • Pytalk

    Pytalk, was a 2 hour workshop conducted by Rutanshu Jhaveri on 19th October, 2016. Pytalk, was all about using speech recognition tools in Python.

    Students learnt to apply these tools to problems around them.

    After on machine learning was incorporated to perform sentiment analysis on any given speech. A thorough understanding was given to students regarding the Python.

  • Webster

    Scrapping the Internet for various resources? Tired of searching what you want from a webpage?

    Webster was all about providing a solution to such problems. A 2 hour classroom event, conducted on 12th October, 2016, by Mayur Shashtri.

    Webster was all about getting what you need on the internet using the Python Beautiful Soup library, which helped display the parsed data.
